## Objection Handling — eCommerce Specific

**"Our customers prefer talking to a real person"**
> They prefer getting an answer in 1 second over waiting on hold for 12 minutes. The brands we work with see no increase in Trustpilot complaints after going live — often the opposite.

**"We use Gorgias / Zendesk for everything"**
> We integrate with both. The AI handles phone calls; Gorgias handles tickets. They work in parallel — every call gets logged as a ticket automatically.

**"We're too small"**
> If you're getting 500+ calls a month and 60% are order status, you're the exact right size. Our Starter plan costs less than one agent's monthly salary.

**"What about returns — those are complicated"**
> We handle the 80% of return calls that are standard: customer confirms their order number, reason, and we log the return request in Shopify and send them a confirmation. Exceptions go to your team.

**"We tried a chatbot and it was awful"**
> This is voice, not chat. Aria sounds human, handles natural conversation, and knows when to escalate. Call +44 20 7946 0000 right now — 60 seconds and you'll see the difference.
