## OUTREACH TIPS

**Volume target this week:** 20 personalised messages per day. At a ~5% reply rate, that's 7 conversations from 140 outreach messages over the week.

**Best times to send LinkedIn messages:** Tuesday–Thursday, 8–9am or 12–1pm. Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons.

**Best times to send cold email:** Tuesday 8am or Thursday 8am consistently outperforms other slots in B2B.

**Personalisation minimum:** The first sentence must be specific to them. Generic openers ("Hope you're well", "I came across your profile") kill response rates.

**Don't follow up more than twice.** Three touches max (original + Day 3 + Day 7). After that, move on and circle back in 90 days.
