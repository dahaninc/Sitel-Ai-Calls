## HOW TO POSITION PRICING IN CONVERSATION

### "Is this cheaper than hiring?"

The comparison isn't "Sitel vs. one agent." It's "Sitel vs. your current call centre cost."

A single customer service agent in the UK costs:
- Salary: £22,000–£28,000/year (£1,833–£2,333/month)
- Employer NI: £2,600–£3,300/year
- Pension (minimum 3%): £660–£840/year
- Training, management overhead, HR: ~£3,000/year
- Recruitment (when they leave — average tenure 14 months): £1,500–£3,000 per hire
- **Total annual cost per agent: £29,760–£37,440**
- **Monthly: £2,480–£3,120**

Our Growth plan at £3,400/month replaces the equivalent of 1.5 agents — while handling 3x the call volume, 24/7, with no sick days, no bad days, and no resignation letters.

### "What if it doesn't work?"

The 3-month minimum protects us both — it takes 30 days to optimise an agent properly and start seeing real deflection numbers. Any vendor who doesn't require at least this doesn't believe in their own product.

That said: if the agent doesn't reach 50% call deflection by Day 60, we waive Month 3's fee. No arguments. We stand behind the results.
