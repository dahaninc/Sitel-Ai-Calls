## THE SOLUTION

### What Sitel AI Deploys for [CLIENT COMPANY]

We build and manage a custom AI voice agent — named and branded to your business — that sits on your existing phone number (or a new dedicated line) and handles inbound customer calls automatically.

**The agent handles:**

- Order status enquiries ("Where is my order?" / "When will it arrive?")
- Returns and refund requests (initiates the return, sends confirmation)
- Delivery FAQs (delivery windows, cutoff times, areas covered)
- Complaint de-escalation (acknowledgement, resolution flow, goodwill offers)
- Post-purchase queries (tracking, damaged goods, wrong items)

**The agent does not handle:**

- Complex disputes or legal queries (these escalate to a human immediately)
- Fraud or account security issues (escalated)
- Any scenario requiring payment processing (escalated)
- Situations where the customer explicitly requests a human (escalated within 5 seconds)
